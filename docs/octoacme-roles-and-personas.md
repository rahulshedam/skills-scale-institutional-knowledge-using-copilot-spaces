# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and ensure the product delivers a great user experience. They collaborate with product managers and developers to create designs that balance usability, accessibility, and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Design user interfaces that meet accessibility standards
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Maintain design systems and style guides
- Iterate designs based on user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, accessible designs
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Maintain design consistency across the product
- Increase user satisfaction and engagement metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with product and engineering teams
- User research findings and persona documentation
- Design handoff documentation and component specifications
- Collaboration with developers during implementation
- Feedback sessions with stakeholders and users

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and observability systems that enable reliable, scalable software delivery. They bridge development and operations to ensure smooth, automated releases.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Ensure security best practices in deployment and infrastructure
- Support incident response and system reliability
- Automate repetitive operational tasks

### Goals
- Maximize deployment frequency and reliability
- Minimize downtime and mean time to recovery (MTTR)
- Ensure scalability and cost-efficiency of infrastructure
- Enable developers to deploy safely and independently

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure planning sessions with engineering leads
- Incident post-mortems and on-call handoffs
- Documentation of runbooks and operational procedures
- Security and compliance reviews
- Performance and cost optimization reports

---

## Technical Writer/Documentation Specialist

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain clear, accurate documentation that helps users, developers, and stakeholders understand and effectively use the product. They translate complex technical concepts into accessible content.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API documentation, and tutorials
- Collaborate with developers and product managers to understand features
- Ensure documentation accuracy and consistency
- Create templates and style guides for documentation
- Manage documentation versioning and releases
- Gather feedback to improve documentation quality

### Goals
- Reduce support tickets through comprehensive documentation
- Enable users and developers to self-serve information
- Maintain documentation that is accurate, up-to-date, and accessible
- Improve onboarding experience for new users and team members

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with product and engineering teams
- Content planning sessions aligned with product roadmap
- Feedback collection from users and support teams
- Collaboration with UX designers on in-product help
- Documentation release notes and update announcements

---

## Customer Success/Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Success/Support Leads ensure customers derive maximum value from the product and receive timely, effective support. They advocate for customer needs, coordinate issue resolution, and drive customer satisfaction and retention.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and resolve customer issues and inquiries
- Escalate complex issues to engineering with clear reproduction steps
- Track support metrics and identify common pain points
- Create and maintain support knowledge base articles
- Conduct customer onboarding and training sessions
- Provide product feedback to inform roadmap priorities

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce response and resolution times
- Identify and advocate for product improvements based on customer feedback
- Enable customers to succeed with minimal friction

### Typical Communication
- Daily ticket triage and customer interactions
- Weekly syncs with product and engineering on top issues
- Escalation reports with detailed issue context
- Customer feedback summaries and feature requests
- Training sessions and onboarding documentation
